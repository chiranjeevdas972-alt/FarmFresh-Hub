Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Welcome to FarmFresh Hub. We are dedicated to maintaining robust privacy protections for our users. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us and to assist you in making informed decisions when using our Livestock & Fresh Food Management Platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect "Non-Personal Information" and "Personal Information." Personal Information includes your email, name, business title, and phone number, which you assist us with during account setup or inquiry forms. Non-Personal Information includes tracking data regarding cage numbers, mortality curves, POS invoices, feed logs, and general livestock inventories saved locally or synced to our secure Firebase Cloud Database.

2. How We Use and Share Information

Except as otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy, we do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share for marketing purposes your Personal Information without your consent. We do use Personal Information to communicate with you and grant access to the secure layers of FarmFresh Hub.

3. Cloud Storage & Firebase Audits

Your authentication tokens and database transactions are stored behind certified identity verification gates utilizing Google Firebase Auth and cloud-hosted Firestore instances configured with strict Attribute-Based Access Control (ABAC).

4. Your Rights Under GDPR & CCPA

Users have the right to inspect, update, or permanently delete all recorded logs, personal accounts, and store inventories at any point from within the accounts preferences console.
